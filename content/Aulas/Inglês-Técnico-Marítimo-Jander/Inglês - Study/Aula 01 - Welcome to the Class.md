---

# 1. VERBO TO BE

|Inglês|Português|
|---|---|
|I am (I’m)|Eu sou / estou|
|You are (You’re)|Você é / está|
|He is (He’s)|Ele é / está|
|She is (She’s)|Ela é / está|
|They are (They’re)|Eles são / estão|

---

## Bizu

- AM → I
    
- IS → He / She
    
- ARE → You / They
    

---

# 2. NEGATIVO

👉 Só adicionar **NOT**

- I am not
    
- She is not / She isn’t
    
- They are not / They aren’t
    

---

## Bizu

👉 Nunca muda ordem — só adiciona NOT

---

# 3. PERGUNTAS

👉 Inverte o verbo

- You are OK → Are you OK?
    
- She is a teacher → Is she a teacher?
    

---

## Bizu

👉 VERBO vem primeiro

---

# 4. RESPOSTAS CURTAS

- Are you OK? → Yes, I am
    
- Is she a teacher? → No, she isn’t
    

---

## Bizu

👉 Copia a pergunta

---

# 5. VOCABULÁRIO DE SALA

## Palavras

- teacher = professor
    
- student = aluno
    
- book = livro
    
- notebook = caderno
    
- pen = caneta
    
- pencil = lápis
    
- chair = cadeira
    
- table = mesa
    

---

## Frases importantes

- Can I go to the toilet? → Posso ir ao banheiro?
    
- Can you repeat, please? → Pode repetir?
    
- I don’t understand → Não entendi
    
- I have a question → Tenho uma pergunta
    
- I’ve finished → Terminei
    
- What page is it? → Que página é?
